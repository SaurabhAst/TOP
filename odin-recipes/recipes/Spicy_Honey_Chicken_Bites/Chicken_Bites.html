Spicy Honey Chicken Bites

HOME

Description And How To Cook

Ingredients:

for the chicken:

  • 250g boneless chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • ½ cup cornstarch (for coating)
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp garlic powder
  • Oil (for frying)

for the sauce

  • 2 tbsp honey 🍯
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (adjust for spice level)
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water (slurry for thickness)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Chicken In a bowl, mix chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Dip each piece in beaten egg, then coat with cornstarch for crispiness.
  2. Fry Until Crispy Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Fry the chicken pieces until golden brown and crispy (about 5-6 minutes). Remove and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  3. Make the Sauce In a pan, heat a little oil and sauté minced garlic until fragrant. Add honey, soy sauce, hot sauce, vinegar, and chili flakes. Stir well, then pour in the cornstarch slurry and cook until the sauce thickens.
  4. Toss & Serve Add the crispy chicken to the sauce and toss to coat evenly. Garnish with sesame seeds or chopped green onions if desired.
  5. 🍽️ Enjoy! Best served with rice, noodles, or as a snack! 😋
