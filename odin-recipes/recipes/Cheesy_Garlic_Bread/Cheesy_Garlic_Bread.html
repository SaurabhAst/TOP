Cheesy Garlic Bread recipe

HOME

Ingredients:

For the Garlic Butter Spread:

  • 4 slices of bread (French baguette or regular bread)
  • 3 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp oregano
  • ½ tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Salt to taste

for the cheeze topping

  • ½ cup mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • 2 tbsp parmesan cheese (optional, for extra flavor)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix softened butter, minced garlic, oregano, chili flakes, parsley, and a pinch of salt.
  2. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly on the bread slices. Sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan cheese generously on top.
  3. reheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Place the bread slices on a baking tray and bake for 8-10 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. For extra crispiness, broil for 1-2 minutes at the end.
  4. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, and enjoy with a side of marinara sauce! 🍕
